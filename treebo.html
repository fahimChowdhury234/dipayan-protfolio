The biggest beauty campaign of 2024: Celebrating Anniversary.

Embarking on a website redesign journey, our primary aim is to reimagine the user experience, particularly in the realm of property booking. We're not just focusing on revamping existing features but also introducing new ones to simplify the process. Furthermore, this project is an opportunity to infuse our platform with a fresh visual style, ensuring that every interaction resonates with elegance and functionality.

Objectives

Enhancing the Booking Experience: Revolutionizing the booking journey involved meticulous attention to detail, integrating enticing property cards featuring unbeatable deals and employing psychological triggers for user engagement. We restructured property cards for enhanced data consumption efficiency, coupled with improved imagery organization and navigation options.

Optimizing Navigation and Content Flow: To streamline user interaction, we meticulously optimized scroll performance and restructured content presentation for aesthetic appeal on both search and property detail pages. We ensured content hierarchy integrity on property detail widgets and maintained consistent font sizes across the website.

Introducing Innovative Features: We introduced captivating new features, including dedicated offer sections on the homepage, compelling testimonial segments, and informative blogs. Moreover, we expanded room selection options on property detail pages and integrated a map view feature for property search and exploration of nearby attractions

Evolving Visual Identity:  A comprehensive visual redesign journey involved revisiting every aspect of the user experience, elevating it to meet industry benchmarks. We meticulously crafted a design component library from scratch to uphold visual consistency across all touchpoints.  

My Role

As the lead designer, I spearheaded collaboration efforts with key stakeholders including leaders, project managers, and the development team. Our journey began with an extensive discovery phase, encompassing heuristic and competitive analysis, user flow mapping, wireframing, and visual design exploration

Research

During the research phase, I conducted both heuristic and competitive analyses to evaluate our product's usability and market positioning. The heuristic analysis identified key usability strengths and weaknesses, guiding our design decisions. Simultaneously, the competitive analysis provided insights into competing products, helping refine our own strategy and enhance user experience.

Mapping it down

With a clear understanding of our project's needs, we crafted empathy maps for our personas to delve into their motivations. These insights informed our user stories, ensuring comprehensive requirement capture. Subsequently, we designed the information architecture to lay a solid foundation for the user experience.
