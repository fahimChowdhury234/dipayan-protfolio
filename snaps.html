Designs Across Projects: A Glimpse into My Portfolio

Here's a glimpse into the diverse array of projects I've recently undertaken, showcasing both my current endeavors and past accomplishments. Each project spans a wide range of subjects, offering a comprehensive overview of my professional interests and expertise.

Designing Loyalty Experience for E-commerce

The loyalty program for a beauty e-commerce brand was truly a standout project, showcasing the adage that good things indeed take time. Over a period of six months, we meticulously fine-tuned every aspect of the program, ensuring it met the highest standards. The design phase, which followed, spanned two months and involved a dedicated team of two. We're immensely proud of the final result and how seamlessly it aligns with our vision.

Crafting new ways to shop Online

Looks Studio is a VR-enabled solution that has been a resounding success among users. This innovative feature has significantly boosted user engagement, resulting in a notable increase in conversions. True to its name, Looks Studio allows users to virtually try on products before making a purchase, providing them with a realistic feel for how the product will look on them.

The Big Bold Visual that took over instagram

Tribe is an influencer-led program that specifically targets a unique set of users who identify with the tribe's values and lifestyle. The essence of the project was all about capturing the vibrant and celebratory spirit of the tribe members, who exude playfulness and joyfulness. With an omnichannel presence, we had the opportunity to fully immerse users in the bold visual language of the brand, making it stand out prominently across various platforms.

Personalising it Just for you

The personalisation project stands out as one of the most captivating endeavors I've undertaken to date. Collaborating with the Google Analytics team and Dynamic Yield, we embarked on a journey of experimentation, testing multiple scenarios to enhance user experiences. The iterative process of design and testing kept me constantly engaged and on my toes, ensuring that we were consistently refining our approach. The tangible impact our efforts had on the organization was significant, making it a rewarding and fulfilling experience.

Pre-order a EV online

Crafted an innovative pre-order kiosk application tailored for offline digital engagement, implementing an omnichannel approach. This user-friendly platform enables seamless vehicle pre-orders while providing comprehensive financial details. Notably, it incorporates a unique environmental calculator, allowing users to assess their carbon footprint
